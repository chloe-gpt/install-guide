6/10/25: Update 16.1.0 and Downgrades

Due to update 16.1.0, the usual Steam downgrade method does not work. Follow the steps below before continuing with your OS- and platform-specific guide. Epic Games users do NOT need to follow these steps and can do the normal downgrade method.

Windows

Press Windows+R and enter the following command.

steam://open/console

In the console that appears, enter the following command.

download_depot 945360 945361 8040785713895232554

Your downgraded folder will be at C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\content\app_945360\depot_945361.

Linux

Open your terminal emulator of choice and enter the following command.

steam steam://open/console

In the console that appears, enter the following command.

download_depot 945360 945361 8040785713895232554

Your downgraded folder will be at /home/[USERNAME]/.local/share/Steam/ubuntu12_32/steamapps/content/app_945360/depot_945361.

Finishing Up

You can move the downgraded folder somewhere more convenient if you wish, and then proceed to install using that folder.

If your game crashes, doesn't load mods, or another issue occurs, join our Discord and make a ticket in the "#tour-support" channel for assistance.
